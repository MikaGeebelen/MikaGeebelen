Cave Invaders

The project

You play as a monster defending your lair against an incoming invasion of enemies that want to steal your treasure. The game works with endless waves. The invading armies will try to get as far into your dungeon as possible to try to reach the door to your treasure. You will get a score based on the number of enemies killed and waves you have survived, this score will be used for a leaderboard. If the army manages to break the door to your treasure room, it is Game Over.

The game is made in C#-unity and is available on itch.io.

My contribution

I created all logic related to the main character, the ladder logic for the enemies, the minimap and implemented all animations.
